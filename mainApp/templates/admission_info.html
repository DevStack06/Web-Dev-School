Class-XI (11th)

Prayas program is to provide quality education to bright and meritorious students from Schedule Caste (SCs), Schedule Tribes (STs) and Other Backward caste (OBC) and students from naxal affected areas so that they are able to secure their career by securing selection in national level competitive examinations. Seats for students are reserved in the following pattern: ST(50%), SC(20%), OBC(20%) and General 10%.So the selection procedure for Students of XI in Boys Prayas Raipur is done by an Entrance Exam followed by Counseling. After successfully completion class-X(10th) from any medium students can apply for Prayas.Student can collect the form of prayas from their District Office/Govt. Schools.There is no separate procedure for selection of Raipur Prayas but from all the aplicants top students will be selected. This depends upon their performance in 10th exam and enterance exam, Domicile Cerificate (Chhattisgarh) and cast.

Class-IX (9th)
