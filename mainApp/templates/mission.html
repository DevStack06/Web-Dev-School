Institute > Mission and Vission

Mission- PRAYAS has been founded with a vision to give quality coaching to IIT, NEET and AIIMS aspirants who belongs to the places affected by naxalism. At PRAYAS we have expert faculties who are either from IIT or NIT which is a first of its kind initiated by CG Government. Smart classes, scientific and interactive teaching methods, doubt clinic and course pre-planner make it stand out of the crowd.

Vision- The key objective of Prayas program is to provide quality education to bright and meritorious students from Schedule Caste (SCs), Schedule Tribes (STs) and Other Backward caste (OBC) and students from naxal affected areas so that they are able to secure their career by securing selection in national level competitive examinations. Seats for students are reserved in the following pattern: ST(50%), SC(20%), OBC(20%) and General 10%.We sincerely believe that education is not learning of facts instead it is a training which makes a mind to think and imagine. That is the reason we use technology & innovative methods which allows students to think more and memorise less which will help them to excel in whatever field they choose.
