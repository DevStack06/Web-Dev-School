PRAYAS, Raipur is committed to provide quality coaching to IIT, NEET and AIIMS aspirants who belongs to the places affected by naxalism. PRAYAS has expert faculties who have completed their higher education either from IIT or NIT which is a first of its kind initiated by CG Government. Smart classes, scientific and interactive teaching methods, doubt clinic and course pre-planner make it stand out of the crowd.

The keen objective of Prayas program is to provide quality education to bright and meritorious students from Schedule Caste (SCs), Schedule Tribes (STs) and Other Backward caste (OBC) and students from naxal affected areas so that they are able to secure their career by securing selection in national level competitive examinations.

We at Prayas sincerely believe that education is not learning of facts instead it is a training which makes a mind to think and imagine. That is the reason we use technology and innovative methods which allows students to think more and memorise less which will help them to excel in whatever field they choose.
