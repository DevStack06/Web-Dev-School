Computer Lab

This is our Computer lab.There are 60 computer runs on windows 10. Students are allowed to use these labs for computer related task,fill there online forms during exam time. Students need to take permission from lab assistent and after that they can use alloted system. Students, faculty and staff have access to the computer labs, which provide the tools and technologies to produce websites, edit papers, complete class assignments, communicate via email, conduct data analyses and access library resources. Microsoft Windows software is available for word processing, statistics, spreadsheets and database management.

Chemistry Lab

We have chemistry lab.Bubbling test tubes, explosions, and color-changing liquids are often what students think about when they hear the word ''chemistry.'' Experimental chemistry can help students explore and apply concepts about chemistry. The general chemistry laboratory is designed to support and illustrate chemical concepts studied in the lecture portion of the course, as well as to introduce important laboratory techniques and encourage analytical thinking. Students are allowed to perform experiment under only instructor's supervision. This lab is also use for students final lab exam.

Physics Lab

We have physics lab in which students can perform various experiments and explore different componets. The Purpose of the Laboratory. Physics is an experimental science. The theoretical concepts and relationships introduced in the lecture part of the course describe the general nature and behavior of real phenomena. Students are allowed perform their experiments under instructor supervision.Same lab will also use for their final physics experiments exams.
